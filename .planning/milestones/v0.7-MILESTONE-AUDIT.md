## Tech Debt

### Pre-existing Structural Limitations

**Postcondition strengthening tautology (behavioral_subtyping.rs:302-309)**
`encode_postcondition_strengthening_vc` encodes `(not (=> trait_ensures trait_ensures))` — always UNSAT. Impl postcondition contracts are not extracted for comparison. This is sound (never produces false verification) but incomplete (doesn't detect postcondition LSP violations). Documented with "For now" comment. Affects the completeness of TRT-01 and TRT-05 postcondition direction.

**Sealed trait is_sealed flag unused in SMT encoding**
`detect_sealed_trait` correctly computes `is_sealed` from HIR visibility (Phase 41), but `generate_subtyping_script` doesn't branch on it. Sum-type encoding variant not yet implemented. TRT-04 detection works; encoding differentiation is future work.
